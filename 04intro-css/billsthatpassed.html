HB 77 – Flag Display Amendments

Status:
Became law without Governor's signature
Summary:
Limits display of non-official flags (e.g., Pride flags) in schools and government buildings

SB 178 – Classroom Device Restrictions

Status:
Signed into law
Summary:
Bans most student phone use in classrooms; schools can opt in with exceptions

HB 300 – Mail-In Voting ID Requirements

Status:
Signed into law
Summary:
Requires voters to use the last 4 digits of a state ID instead of a signature on mail-in ballots starting in 2026

HB 420 – Halogen Emissions Regulation

Status:
Signed into law
Summary:
Begins state-level monitoring and regulation of halogen emissions that worsen air quality

SB 500 – Utah State Park Funding

Status:
Signed into law
Summary:
Allocates additional funding for maintenance and expansion of Utah state parks
