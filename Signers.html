Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, first to sign the Declaration of Independence.
Benjamin Franklin
One of the Founding Fathers, known for his contributions to science and diplomacy.
Thomas Jefferson
Main author of the Declaration of Independence and third President of the United States.
John Adams
Second President of the United States and a key advocate for independence.
